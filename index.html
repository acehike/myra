An Abode in the city

Connectivity that puts you minutes from everywhere. MYRA’s location is at the heart of a major URA initiative to intensify the greening of Serangoon. A mere kilometre away from MYRA is a new 1.6km-long park connector – the Bidadari Greenway and Bidadari Park. The park’s masterplan envisions it to be a car-lite and cyclist-friendly community with seamless connectivity to Bishan, Kallang, Serangoon, Ang Mo Kio, Seletar, Sengkang, all the way up to Punggol.

Potong Pasir is strategically situated mid-way between the Central Business District and the east; surrounded by Pan Island Expressway (PIE), Central Expressway (CTE) and Kallang-Paya Lebar Expressway (KPE). The Potong Pasir MRT station located 150m away from MYRA connects its residents easily throughout the island.

FACILITIES


MYRA’s central feature is its series of interconnected pools, anchored by a 25m lap pool, all of which create multiple water edges with each edge opening up to a different space, and the luxury of different experiences.

At the north-east end lies a quiet zone, with its distinctive pool overflow feature designed as rivulets of water. Here, there are decks with sunbath loungers, a poolside walk, a quiet sitting area, an alfresco BBQ dining area and even a ‘Chill Deck’.
